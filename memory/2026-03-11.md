## Proactive Work — 2026-03-11 18:14 ET

**Action**: Fixed agentcash crash when paying v1 x402 servers (PR #261 on Merit-Systems/agentcash)

**Root cause**: Mercury402 (and likely other third-party servers) encode a flat v1 PaymentRequirements object in the `Payment-Required` header (`{scheme, network, amount, payTo, ...}`) instead of the v2 wrapper (`{x402Version, accepts[], resource}`). The x402 core library's `decodePaymentRequiredHeader` just does JSON.parse with no normalization. `handleX402Payment` then crashes at `paymentRequired.accepts[0]` because `accepts` is undefined.

**Fix**: Added `normalizePaymentRequired()` in `fetch-with-payment.ts` — detects flat v1 objects (has scheme/network/amount at top level, no accepts array) and wraps them in v2 format. Build, types:check, and lint all pass.

**Context**: Issue filed by Mercury402 operator (Dustin) 2 hours ago. With the $100K AgentCash giveaway driving new users, third-party x402 servers need to work. This is the kind of bug that makes the ecosystem look broken.
